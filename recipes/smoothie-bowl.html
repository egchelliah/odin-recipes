Smoothie Bowl

Description

On a hot summers day, a smoothie always cools me down. Sometimes we like to change it up for the kids, so we make them smoothie bowls, and they love it! A cold and creamy smoothie base, topped of with crunchy granola, chia seeds and fruit. The perfect snack on a hot day or a great way to kick off your morning.

Ingredients:

  • Frozen strawberries
  • Vanilla Yogurt
  • Fruit (whatever fruit you prefer)
  • Chia Seeds
  • Granola

Steps:

  1. Blend the frozen strawberries, vanilla yogurt, ice, and honey
  2. Pour it into a cereal bowl, and smooth out the top with a spoon.
  3. Pour some granola to one side. In this case, I used vanilla, pumpkin seed granola, and it adds the perfect crunch!
  4. Pour a little row of chia seeds.
  5. Start placing your fruit in the remaing space. In our case, we used really sweet blackberries and some blood orange wedges.
